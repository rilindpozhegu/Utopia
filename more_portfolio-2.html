Utopia
Portfolio

Utopia designed, managed and produced the 2016 Annual Awards Night for JA Resorts and Hotels at the Jebel Ali Resort in Dubai. The glittering evening featured a Hollywood theme, complete with a red carpet entrance, a gala dinner, awards ceremony, entertainment and a lot of glamour.

The guests were welcomed at the event venue by four hostesses provided by GEM’s hostess division, Premiere Hostess. There was a candy buffet and cocktail bar open to the guests.

The night featured a number of entertainment acts organised and managed by GEM Entertainers, GEM group’s entertainment division.

Utopia also designed the concept and the stage, along with providing complete technical production expertise, photography and videography.
