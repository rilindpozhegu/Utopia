Utopia
Portfolio

Utopia were fortunate to be awarded the contract from a top consultancy firm in the UAE, to organise a Casino Royale party for over 250 people in April 2016.

The event started with a welcome breakfast and then the guests were whisked away by 25 stretch limos to Nikki Beach Club. Here they had half of the beach club exclusively set aside for their group and a fantastic violinist played for them alongside a DJ for the afternoon.

All guests were then transported by stretch limos to Ras Al Khaimah to the Waldorf Astoria hotel.

After a one hour drinks reception, the limos took the clients to a large yacht moored in Al Hamra Marina where their evening began. They were greeted at the Vault entrance by Bond girls dressed in stunning Gold catsuits.
